Our Services

  • LEXURY BLEND

    Lexury provides 100% Arabica. Our offerings are premium value-added coffee products. We believe that our customers deserve the best coffee product and we carefully source top quality coffee beans and process with our unique roasting profile to consistently provide a quality product. Understanding the unique needs of service providers, Lexury has developed a service package. Our service package is very convenient to use and easy to store.

  • SINGLE ORIGIN

    Ethiopian coffee brings complex flavour profiles that are hard to find elsewhere. Ethiopia is home to the most genetically diverse varieties of Coffee Arabica.This includes some of highly celebrated coffee varieties such as Yirgachefe, Harar, Sidama, Limmu, Jimma and others. Lexury will supply these special and single origin coffee for the export market

  • SUPPORT

    Sourcing direct from smallholder farmers to deliver fairly-traded specialty and premium coffee to consumers worldwide, LEXURY COFFEE is roasted and packed at source in Ethiopia, the origin of the best coffee in the world. Supporting sustainable livelihoods and biodiversity conservation, this husband and wife team is creating change through coffee. So, come and TASTE ETHIOPIA through our coffees.
